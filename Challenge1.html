My Poem

Charles Bukowski [1920-1994] was one of the most famous of the American poets of his time. He was first published in his 20s., but
gave up serious writing for the world of work and bars. He spent a lot of time roaming from job to job living in rooming houses from the East coast to the West coast before joining the United States Postal Service in Los Angeles.
His life at that time bordered on insanity and death, two prevalent themes in his writing.
